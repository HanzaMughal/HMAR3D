📋 How to deploy & use

  1. Compile your marker image at MindAR Compiler ↗ → save as assets/targets/targets.mind
  2. Upload this entire project folder to Vercel ↗ or Netlify ↗ (free & HTTPS)
  3. Copy your live URL (e.g. https://burger-ar.vercel.app/ar.html)
  4. Paste it above and click Generate
  5. Download & print the QR code on your menus or posters
  6. Users scan → website opens → 3D burger appears! 🍔✨
